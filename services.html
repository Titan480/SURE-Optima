How We Work Together

Every engagement follows a clear process. This is what working with SURE Optima looks like from the first conversation to active execution.

01

Discovery

We start with a conversation. No assumptions, no pitch. The goal is to understand your situation, what you'd like to achieve, and whether there is a genuine fit.

→
02

Alignment and Scope

If there is a fit, we define the work together. What are we solving, what does success look like, and what is the path to get there. This becomes the foundation of the engagement.

→
03

Documentation

We formalize the engagement with the right paperwork for the situation. NDA, Statement of Work, or a simpler letter of engagement depending on scope and complexity.

→
04

Kickoff

Work begins. Roles are defined, cadence is set, and we get after it.

Ways of Working

Engagements are structured to match the work, not the other way around. Here are the most common models.

Project Based

A defined scope of work with clear deliverables and a timeline. Best for specific problems, assessments, strategic plans, or one-time execution needs.

Retainer

Ongoing access and support for a set number of hours or days per month. Best for companies that need consistent strategic input or fractional leadership without a full-time commitment.

Fractional Leadership

Embedded, part-time executive or program leadership. Best for companies navigating growth, transition, or a capability gap that requires senior-level presence without a full-time hire.

What to Expect

Directness

You will always know where things stand. No sugarcoating, no runaround. If something is not working, we say so.

Adaptability

The process is a starting point. We adjust to your culture, your pace, and your reality as the engagement evolves.

A Small Client Roster by Design

We work with a limited number of clients at any given time. That is not a constraint, it is a commitment to doing the work well.
